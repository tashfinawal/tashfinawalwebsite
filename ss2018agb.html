GUIDELINES

Here are the guidelines that we brainstormed and agreed upon as a group (relatively)

  • Cost of gift should be $10, max $20 including tax
  • Creativity > Costliness; try to make the gift more meaningful/sentimental and less about the money that went into it
  • No wishlists; the recieving party should be not only surprised of who their Santa is, but what they are recieving
  • Gifts cannot have been purchased with Brazio, which is Brazil's Cryptocurrency (because it's worthless)
